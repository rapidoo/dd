                       THE BARBER OF SILVERYMOON
             People have been disappearing at night in the city of Silverymoon. Some vanish entirely,
                    leaving behind whispered rumors of fiends or other evil creatures having spirited
          them away. Others return strangely altered, with their memories of having been kidnapped
                wiped clean and their minds strangely dulled—and always with remarkable haircuts.




                                               An adventure for characters of 4th to 6th level
